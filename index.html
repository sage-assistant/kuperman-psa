Why We're Worried

The Warning Signs

They're only 13 months apart. They've essentially been together since birth. At what point does "siblings" become "life partners who happen to share DNA"?

06

According to Harvard Magazine, they "look almost like twins." This level of compatibility should be shared with a non-related partner.

Why This Would Be Wrong
